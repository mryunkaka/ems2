# Code of Conduct

EMS2 follows the Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct, version 2.1.

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project respectful, professional, and free from harassment.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Focus feedback on code, documentation, security, and maintainability.
- Assume sensitive operational contexts and avoid exposing private data.
- Accept maintainer decisions when they are made to protect users, privacy, or project scope.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, discrimination, or threats.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Sharing real patient, employee, applicant, salary, credential, or medical data.
- Repeated off-topic disruption after maintainer requests to stop.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject contributions, or block participants who violate this code.
